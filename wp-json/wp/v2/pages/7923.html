Pronounced “Zick-Zax”.<\/div>\n

These hawkbill style blades were made from the bones of dragons. They have gradually vanished over time, as they do eventually get old and break. There is no known way of repairing them. Elite Dragonborn warriors used these, and it is thought they were a gift from their sires. Although they could be used by anyone, their power was amplified in the hands of the dragonborn. They would also go out of their way to reclaim these blades from those not of the race.\n<\/p>\n
