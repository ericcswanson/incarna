                  Essence Corruption<\/h2>\n

                  A character’s essence can be corrupted, inflicted with disease, made toxic, and their very nature (alignment, codes of conduct, etc.) be changed. <\/p>\n

                  <\/span>Essence Taint<\/span><\/h2>\n

                  A target Essence is has its nature twisted by outside forces. It becomes “tainted” – and their aura will begin to read in ways in which the possessor cannot control – good shows as evil, evil as good, creature type, alive as dead, etc. <\/p>\n
