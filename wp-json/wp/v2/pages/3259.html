<\/span>Gifts: Channeling Divinity<\/span><\/h2>\n

Gifts are powers granted only to followers directly by the deity, used by the Channel Divinity<\/b> power. They are petitioned for in acts of supplication part of the journey of faith. They may conviction in order to petition for and use. A gift may mimic any other faith power, trait, ability, magical or other power.\n<\/p>\n

<\/span>Learning Gifts for Channel Divinity<\/span><\/h4>\n

The follower spends time in supplication equal to [gift’s min. conviction] x 1 week<\/span> in worship petitioning their deity for the power to use the gift, after which they
\nmake a Wisdom check which will indicate if the deity grants the gift.
