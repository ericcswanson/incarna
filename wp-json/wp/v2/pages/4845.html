They are more likely to solve problems locally, not involving Justicars and High King\u2019s Rangers – except for the contest which sends them away. The Deeping is weak in the region, but the Feywild boundaries are thin in many places. The underdark realms normally find no purchase, but the Drow have spent the last 300 years excavating an ancient Ducateon fortress that was washed out \u2013 attacked by elemental magics of water, ice, mist and fog (over a thousand years ago \u2013 attacked by strange shadow trolls thought to have been some sort of allies of the Lich Lords)\u2026 there are still frozen trolls that come alive once more when unfrozen.
