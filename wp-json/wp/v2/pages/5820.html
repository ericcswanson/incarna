The Seaborne Islands still have a plentiful supply of this deadly substance, and it is a major export for the Green Council families. It can be found I the lairs of sea serpents (rare), and in reefs off some of the more dangerous isles.\n<\/p>\n<\/h2>\n

Properties of Cutfin Coral<\/h2>\n

It absorbs blood and is highly sought after by magi. When cut, it generally causes a longer hemorrhage as the cuts do not close quickly at all. When powdered, it can be mixed with other things to induce lethargy and even a dangerous, coma like sleep. It is also used in remedies to leech blood and cleanse wounds. If inhaled, it causes a numbness and makes it difficult to breathe.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
