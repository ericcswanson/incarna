The MTG play options we have used over the years. The point is to have fun and be creative. Non traditional decks, untried decks, and ad-hoc decks are encouraged. <\/p>\n

Freeform<\/h2>\n

This is the default.<\/b> Basically, you can use any card, from any set (even banned). If its super powerful, dont use more than 1. It must be 60 cards minimum. Unless the use of a Commander is agreed on ahead of time, none are allowed (though other commander zone effects can be used creatively).\n<\/p>\n

Ad-Hoc by Decree<\/h2>\n
    \n
  1. Each player puts a condition on the game. EXAMPLES:\n
      \n
    • Must be 3 colors<\/li>\n
    • No more than 1 of any non basic land card.<\/li>\n
    • Must be mon colored.<\/li>\n
    • One color must be white<\/li>\n
    • No creature with a power greater than 2 may be cast\/<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n<\/li>\n
    • It cannot use a pre-built deck (at least half the deck must be assembled on the spot).<\/li>\n
    • If a player does not have the cards to match the conditions, they must declare it before the deck building begins or within 5 minutes of searching. Such players may ask for an exception to be made if they are close to fulfilling it.<\/li>\n
    • Players have 20 minutes (or an agreed on time) to assemble a deck matching the conditions.<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n<\/ol>\n

      Super Weenie<\/2><\/p>\n
        \n
      1. Nothing can cost more than 1 casting cost.<\/li>\n
      2. No spell may have X in the cost.<\/li>\n
      3. Cycling is allowed to exceed the 1 mana cost.<\/li>\n
      4. Players begin play with 8 cards.<\/li>\n
      5. Once during the game, during their turn, a player may declare a “free draw” and draw a card. This can be accomplished as a sorcery.<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
