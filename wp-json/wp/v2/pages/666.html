      A quick reference for learning some common elements of society and place of the prevailing race\/culture<\/div>\n

      Their land is and culture is organized into strict peerage rule. The Church of Light and King holds sway equally at the top. The nation is most self-reliant and all commerce is highly regulated – expressly to be non-dependant upon other nations. They have little in the way of a navy, except coast hugging and military vessels. The culture is forcibly homogeneous and xenophobic – they are not accepting of diversity and excessive prideful in their militaristic organizations, knights, tradition and conservative values. The church has a well organized education arm, and devout followers of even lesser social standing<\/a> are often allowed a minor education programs<\/a> – which includes indoctrination influencing loyalty and patriotism of nobility and church.\n<\/p>\n

        The people of Umbak are law-abiding and somber. They are devout in their pursuit of the values of the Church of Light – Balthazaar is the nation\u2019s patron god and they are always crusading to spread their faith. Their cities are well organized and they are disciplined craftsmen. Umbakians have an extreme gender bias – women are limited in their roles, but revered as the managers of the home and hearth. They are xenophobic to almost all other outsiders and hostile to non-humans.
