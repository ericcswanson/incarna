    <\/span>D20 Occult Aptitude Mechanics<\/span><\/h3>\n

    Occult Abilities: Intelligence, Charisma (core\/most common)
    \nOccult Group: A Coven; Requires 3+ occultists can combine powers
    \nAll occult abilities manifest as if it were a normal spell for requirements and effects, unless specifically noted. All spells are cast at the Occult Class level, or minimum level needed to cast (whichever is greater). If an aptitude power is not normally usable by the Occult Class, the character can still use it.\n<\/p>\n

      \n
    • Major Occultists<\/b>: These have a deep background of regimine, discipline, and experience. They include: Bard (College of Lore only), Sorcerer, Warlock, or Wizard, Fighter (Eldritch Knight – Occult Chronicle or Minor Pact), Rogue (Arcane Trickster – Occult Chronicle or Minor Pact).<\/li>\n
    • Minor Occultists<\/b>: This represents a tangential, minimal, or passing interest only. These include: Magic Initiate and Ritual Caster that use Wizard\/Bard\/Sorcerer\/Warlock spells with Occult Chronicle or Minor Pact as their source.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n

      <\/span>High Occultist<\/span><\/h4>\n

      Once the Occult Level has exceeded aptitude (5th).<\/p>\n

        \n
      • Bard<\/b>: They gain a wizard school, and Bardic Inspiration is doubled when used resisting spells, +1 Spells Known and +1 Spell Slot<\/li>\n
      • Sorcerer<\/b>: +1 Sorcery Point, +1 Metamagic, Regain +1 Sorcery point per Short Rest, +1 Spell Known<\/li>\n
      • Warlock<\/b>: +1 spells known. +1 Spell Slot and +1 Eldritch Invocation when they get their pact boon OR can use their 6th level warlock powers twice as often. Their curses after they get their pact boon are stronger as well. Bane is always maximum penalty, and Bestow Curse has disadvantage on its save. <\/li>\n
      • Wizard<\/b>: Gains a 2nd level ability from another school (not any tradition – must be a school of magic) at 5th and another at 10th (or the 6th level ability in the first one), +1 cantrip, can use 5 mana to cast Detect Magic, +1 to Arcane Recovery, +1 prepared spell.\n<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n<\/div>\n
