The Drowned Ones are the animations raised in service to Elancil. They are not specific to Gwinnish forces or sects – true clerics of the Storm Queen can call upon the sea to deliver up the servants of the goddess to attack their enemies.\n<\/p>\n

Description<\/b>: The Drowned Ones are characterized by the salt they leave in their footsteps, and the wounds they create. Grave goods are often entwined or still possessed by them.\n<\/div>\n
Combat<\/b>: Brine Burn: Any wound = DC 10 Constitution check or d4\/2 Necrotic and 1 level Exhaustion until Long Rest\n<\/div>\n
