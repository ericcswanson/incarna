4-22-15
\nWe make our way to the door. We say \u201cI am a goblin, open the door\u201d in goblin. We see a shimmer and a veil is lifted. There is a hole in the door.
\nPriestwick and Sherlock come running up and say \u201cWe\u2019ll guard the door after you go in\u201d. (Singen is immediately suspicious).
\nSingen goes first through the door. We see a corridor that has partially collapsed. The footing is treacherous. There are stairs that descend.
\nMeena climbs over the rubble, then Eera, then Sinjin, then Alta. We all make it down the stairs across the rubble.
\nWe hear something behind us and turn to see the skeleton of a dog-like creature.
\nAlta attacks it with her quarter staff, hitting it. She also strikes it with her hand critting him doing max damage.
\nMeena tries to shoot him, but misses.
\nSinjin casts Heroism on Alta.
\nThe skeleton tries to bite Alta, but misses.
\nAlta attacks, missing with her quarter staff, but hitting with her off hand.
\nMeena misses her shot again.
\nEera tries to sling it, but misses.
\nSinjin casts True Strike on Alta.
\nAlta hits her attack.
\nMeena misses again.
\nEera attacks and hit.
\nSinjin casts True Strike on Alta.
\nAlta hits the dog again and it collapses.
\nWe move into the next large room. There are 7 doors with animals depicted on the floor in front of each door. On the floor is a mural of the Sun Lord with suns for eyes, with a Lady on his arm.
\nSinjin moves across the room and tries to open the large door. It is made out of stone, as the outer door was. After great effort, he gets the door open. We see another set of stairs.
\nSinjin goes through the door. A portcullis comes down on top of him. He takes damage and falls back into the room. He uses Second Wind to heal himself almost back to full strength.
\nHe enters a small anti-chamber with a crystal chandelier covered in moss and lichen. He carefully checks out the room, and he falls down into a pit trap.
\nMeena throws him a rope and he climbs up. On the other side of the room is another stairway and another room. This one is larger and painted with yellow suns. There is a gold sun in the middle of the room. There is a skeleton lying in the room with a shining sword.
\nFour skeletons appear in the room with us.
\nOne of them attacks Meena but misses.
\nAnother misses Alta. Alta misses with her staff.
\nAnother misses Sinjin.
\nEera attacks with her scimitar but misses.
\nMeena hits with one attack but misses her second.
\nA skeleton hits Eera.
\nSinjin take out a skeleton with Dissident Whispers. He casts Bardic Inspiration on Meena (2).
\nEera hits with her scimitar.
\nMeena takes out one skeleton and damages another.
\nEera is injured.
\nSinjin casts True Strike and Bardic Inspiration on Alta.
\nAlta hits the skeleton next to her.
\nMeena is hit.
\nEera crits on a skeleton. He takes him out.
\nMeena hits one attack but misses the second.
\nSinjin readies his bow and notches and arrow.
\nAlta moves and hits the last skeleton. She takes him down.
\nSinjin looks at the sword carefully. There are 3 sun inscriptions on the sword.
\nWe decide to heal ourselves and rest the night.
\nSinjin casts Identify on the sword. Blade of the Sun Lord. Upon touching the sword, it sets off a spear trap, but Sinjin is able to dodge it.
\nForged 350 years ago for Ka\u2019Teek. Magical sword. +2 Longsword (+4 against evil creatures). It can only be used by Good alignment. It bonds with the owner and tries to return to him if lost. (Meena)<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
