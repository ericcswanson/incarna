Marines
\nMarines are the elite heavy troops Gwinn deploys. They all can swim, and are taught how to fight on the deck of a moving ship. Even up to their neck in water, they fight without penalties. They follow orders, but are taught how to work independently as well as in a team. <\/p>\n

Soldiers
\nThe average soldier of Gwinn is human, and highly trained like the marines. The marines are made up of soldiers that are good enough. Like marines, they are taught to fight in water and on the deck of a ship. <\/p>\n

Skirmishers
\nSkirmishers are ambush attackers. Like 99% of Gwinns military, they are human. They specialize in athletic and survival ability, and striking from a distance. <\/p>\n
