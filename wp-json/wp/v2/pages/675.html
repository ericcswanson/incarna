      A quick reference for learning some common culture and flavor of the place.<\/div>\n

      Their land is resource rich and the stock and extraction of these is the core of its commerce. Transportation of these is limited to the north with the trade monopoly of the Merchant Cities. The people are diverse and accepting, and have a long tradition of service to the High King and their beloved Duke. Dunstrand has one of the few (albeit small) formal education programs<\/a> in the realms (with Ducal sponsorship) – access is depending on social standing<\/a> and the luck of availability. There is no official religion endorsed, though most follow the precepts of the Wyld Faith<\/a> – except for the Lightbringers<\/a> in East River Run<\/a> and West River Run<\/a> counties.\n<\/p>\n
