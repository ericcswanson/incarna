<\/span>Changes to Existing Backgrounds<\/span><\/h2>\n

<\/span>Noble<\/span><\/h3>\n

The character is, by default, a minor noble. At best, they have their retinue paid for – servants like a groomsman, teamster, etc. They have been given a leg up in the world, but little else. They would be able to borrow against their good name, but they do not have access to fabulous wealth. Social Standing<\/a> should be used to determine any ongoing and starting wealth, training, and asset options.\n<\/p>\n
