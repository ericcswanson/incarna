In the iVerse<\/a>, the Orrish are part of the humanoid Faybrin<\/a> genus. One of the original and oldest races of the cosmos, they are hearty, primitive and adaptive to a high degree. <\/p>\n

The Orrish were originally the Feyrish, along with the Faelen (Human blood) and Faerie (Elven blood). Rebellious and primitive, they have carved out their own place through alliance with evil and violent otherworldly powers and violently reject their shared ancestry. Generally, they view their human cousins as weak chattel, and their elven cousins as mentally deficient in their embrace and transformation to Sylvan entities.\n<\/p>\n
