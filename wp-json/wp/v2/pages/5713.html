Bio:
\nGrace is from the Peregrine family, an old family SW of the city proper of Osmail Mark. They have tilled the land and have about a dozen tenants. Grace\u2019s father \u2013 Sira William Peregrine \u2013 is no knight, but as a land owner, tax payer and ties to the history and support of the baron has been made an official \u201clord\u201d of Peregrine estate and is considered a gentleman. Grace has 2 brothers living and a brother and sister dead. She entered the Baron\u2019s service as a lady-in-waiting for the Baron\u2019s daughter Matilda. In reality, she reported to her mother and father. Matilda was married very young and left \u2013 Grace did not want to leave and offered her services as a young woman to the Baron\u2019s herald, Claude. Over the years she grew in her beauty, seductive techniques and wisdom \u2013 eventually inheriting Claude\u2019s job \u2013 bypassing others with seniority (Adard Helpran and Keom Odway) and making a few political enemies in the process. The baron and his family trust Grace implicitly.
