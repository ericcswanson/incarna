The Deeping (also known as “the deepening”) is a corrupted presence that infuses places deep underground. It generally shuns the light and clean air, and no trace of it will be found within 50 feet of the surface, or within 300 feet of any entrances to the deep underground. This presence is not individually sentient in the way higher order beings are, but it does react to things as a collective, and is predatory towards sentient beings. In some places it establishes a symbiotic relationship with deep dwellers in its shadow – they draw in more creatures for it to feed on, an in return that are unaffected by its embrace. Wherever there is a presence of The Deeping, it will contain several Corrupted Sparks<\/a>. Madness, panic, and lassitude reign in places were The Deeping has found its way in – it feeds on such things to sustain its power.\n<\/p>\n

Effects of The Deeping<\/h3>\n

Constant Vertigo<\/mark> = The primary noticeable constant effect is vertigo. Each time it is entered into and each hour inside its effects, the victim must make a DC 10 Sanity check or suffer 1 level of Exhaustion (never more than a single level imposed from the normal vertigo) and -1 Sanity for 1 hour.\n<\/p>\n

As the caster of the shadow moves away, a thin strand of shadow connects the 2, getting thinner with every step. 10\u2019: DC 20 Perception check to notice; for each 5 feet 1 less DC to notice and for each 10\u2019 after the first the shadow\u2019s owner takes D4\/2 (black) Lightning damage (per 10\u2019). Movement is reduced by 5\u2019 per 20\u2019 distant from the shadow; @ zero the victim cannot travel further but can act in place. Apply light directly to the shadow to free it. <\/li>\n
  • Overwhelming Vertigo<\/b>: The Sanity check is a DC 15 and penalty is 2 levels of Exhaustion.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Protections of The Deeping<\/h3>\n

    Protections are often afforded to creatures who make their home on the edges or in the corruption of The Deeping. If such creatures are evil, and lure others into the terror and death of the deep darkness, the corruption may serve more as a barrier than an envelope – to encourage their predation.\n<\/p>\n

      \n
    • Stygian Steadiness<\/b>: The effects buoy the target, granting them Immunity to normal vertigo and Shadow Clutch effects of The Deeping, and a bonus of +5 to any other resistance checks to its effects. <\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

        Origins of The Deeping<\/h2>\n

        The Deeping is an effect of specific circumstances. It only happens in places where Godswars have happened. When a thoroughly evil and corrupt god is truly killed, and their blood or equivalent leeches into the earth it takes on a new life, draining the life essence from the earth and water around it, corrupting it. The life form it becomes is like a shadowy remnant of the dead god. As it grows, it feeds on the deaths of those in its shadow, corrupting their essence as their souls transcend. As it grows, it anchors portions of itself with the Corrupted Sparks<\/a>. These are points to avoid; they manifest as severe psychic disturbances and opened portals to nightmare realms and Shadowfell. <\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
