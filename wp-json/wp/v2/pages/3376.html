\nMeena takes a potion (8 pts) and gives 1 of hers to Balder.
\nMeena has a +1 Longbow from the now dead Sinjin and gives her standard Longbow to Modax and 10 arrows.
\nRax moves forward into an open area. She hears a scream and a large, harry humanoid (lycanthrope) jumps out at her. She casts Witch Bolt (crits) for full damage.
\nBalder moves next to Rax and attacks the werewolf, hitting it.
\nThe werewolf attacks Balder biting him. Balder makes his save.
\nMeena shoots her longbow, hitting the werewolf.
\nModax casts Magic Missile for 10 pts.
\nRax casts Witches Bolt, but only for 1 pt.
\nBalder crits him, dropping him. He turns into a human.
\nOn the south end of the room is another werewolf and mid-way is a creature that has many potions and possible spell books. He is very hideous (Bekinqua?).
\nBaquinka starts to cast a spell. Modax casts Magic Missile, hitting him.
\nBalder moves next to Baquinka and hit him with his great axe.
\nBaquinka disengages from Balder and disappears.
\nThe werewolf moves up to Balder and attacks, hitting him with a claw, crits with the other claw and Balder drops to the ground. The werewolf starts moving up to the rest of us.
\nMeena shoots the werewolf but it doesn\u2019t seem to do any damage.
\nRax casts Charm person and it is successful. Rax tells us to hold \u201cWe are all friends here\u201d.
\nThe werewolf stands there and seems confused.
\nRax tries to tell the werewolf that we are friends via ESP, but he hears \u201cI have control of him, you do not\u201d and the werewolf comes out of his Charm and starts towards Meena and Modax again.
\nBalder rolls a death save and fails #1.
\nA small vial flies over Balders head and breaks about 10 feet from him and bursts into flame.
\nThe werewolf moves to Modax and attacks, but he misses.
\nMeena attacks the werewolf with the Sun Lord sword hitting and doing some damage. She also hits with her regular shorts word, but it does no damage.
\nModax goes into a rage and attacks the werewolf hitting him.
\nBalder rolls a successful death roll.
\nRax is hit with a small vial which burst into a flash flame. Rax moves without stepping in it.
\nThe werewolf attacks Modax but misses.
\nMeena attacks the werewolf hitting with the Sun Lord blade.
\nRax blasts the werewolf with an Eldritch Blast hitting it.
\nModax attacks with his spear, hitting it for 10 pts.
\nBalder fails the 3rd death roll.
\nThe werewolf misses his attack.
\nMeena hits with the Sun Lord blade.
\nRax moves next to Balder and gives him a healing potion. Balder is up again.
\nModax attacks with his spear, hitting it.
\nBalder uses Second Wind to gain 3 more pts. He moves up and sends a blast of cold breath at the werewolf. He takes half damage.
\nThe werewolf misses his attack on Modax.
\nMeena hits with the Sun Lord blade.
\nRax hits with her Eldritch Blast, critting it, dropping the werewolf.
\nModax moves to Balder and gives him a healing potion.
\nRax tells us where she last saw Baquinka. We head that direction. Another vile lands about 10 feet in front of us.
\nModax moves over and attacks Baqinka with his chilling hand, but misses.
\nBalder moves up and attacks, hitting him.
\nBaqinka steps back, giving Balder an attack of op. who crits him. It kills him.
\nWe find some lab equipment in another alcove.
\nSack of 255 G, 1041 C,
\nLarge book-Tome of Lycanthrope control. (Infernal)
\nRax takes Baqinka\u2019s head.
\nHe is a bearded devil (without a beard).
\nHe had 2 glass balls with liquid inside (like the flaming bombs that were thrown at us).
\n2 Small dolls with stones for heads.
\nWe search the rest of the cave, avoiding another rock trap.
\nThere was another doll on the table. We take it all, including the heads of the werewolves and leave the cave. It is mid-morning. We head back to town quickly.
\nWe present the heads of the people who were werewolves to the Mayor. We tell the sage what we found. The Mayor pays us 100 G each and 500 for the devil. We will draw a map to the cave for another 100 G. The sage pays 150 G for the return of his lab equipment.
\nThe sage tells us the dolls (fetish) control the werewolves. There will still be one werewolf out there somewhere. The sage feels the 4th one has not turned yet.
\nWe will rest and resupply for now.
\nRax identifies the book. It\u2019s a magic tome that radiates evil. Covered in human werewolf skin. It mentions a well-known necromancy. Only can be used by Devils. They can control werewolves by creating a fetish. (14,500 G).<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
