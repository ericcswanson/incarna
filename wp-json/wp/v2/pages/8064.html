In absence of direct regulation or specific overriding instructions by a campaign GM, the general provisions of ‘interim play’ are applied as follows:\n<\/p>\n

    \n
  • No powerful items (mystic or mundane) are introduced into the campaign<\/li>\n
  • No major plot devices are introduced – the scenario must be self contained<\/li>\n
  • No more than few days of time are allowed to elapse in game terms<\/li>\n
  • Very little can be awarded for a single session<\/li>\n
  • No major wealth is introduced into the campaign<\/li>\n
  • No major NPC’s are introduced into the campaign<\/li>\n
  • If all other characters die, any PC belonging to the interim GM and used in the scenario must also perish<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
