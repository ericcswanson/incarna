Garresht Family<\/h2>\n

Patron<\/b>: The King of Umbak; Ikribu, Lord of Light
\nCrest<\/b>:
\nMotto<\/b>: Their motto was “” <\/p>\n

The Garresht family is an old family with lands on the Queen\u2019s Coast of [northern] Umbak. History is mostly silent in terms of great deeds, but their ancestors have for millennium been captains of the vessels taking the crusaders around the world to spread the faith and word of law. They are absolutely loyal to the king of Umbak, and tenacious foes of Gwinn – especially since their expansionist actions have brought them into direct conflict across the channel in Tarmysia. There are several family branches, all settled on either coastal lands or lands to manage for timber to build boats.\n<\/p>\n

Satrole Family<\/h2>\n

Patron<\/b>: The King of Umbak; Ikribu, Lord of Light
\nCrest<\/b>: A black right side, with a yellow cone of daylight expanding from the left side.
\nMotto<\/b>: Their motto was “Daylight in the Darkness” <\/p>\n

The Satrole family is an old family of Umbak. They were a key force is the persistent drives to the north , to settle Dunstrand Vale. Constantly resisted by the Sea Kings, Bronzemen and still by Dunstrandians.
