Transgression means that an action, contemplation, or end result has gone directly against a character’s belief (Code of Conduct, tenets of faith, etc.). Typically both direct and indirect results count as a transgression – depending on whether the such beliefs are adhered to in both letter and spirit. A transgression is an affront to the deeply held beliefs and represents opposition to them. <\/p>\n

Transgression against others beliefs can result in some sort of proclamation against the transgressing entity by civil, religious<\/a>, or organization authorities which has repercussions in the game world. This can result in ostracization, prohibitions, removal of power(s) and\/or actions taken to harm or kill the transgressor. <\/p>\n
