11\/4\/15
\nArena night:
\nBalder, Meena, and Modax are transported to an arena. We are fighting a giant.
\nMeena casts Hail of Thorns and attacks with her longbow. She hits.
\nModax moves back 10 ft. and casts Magic Missile. He casts his meta-magic to hit with Magic Missile again.
\nBalder misses.
\nThe giant attacks Balder, critting on his first attack and hit on the second one.
\nMeena crits and hits on her turn.
\nModax rages and attacks with his spear, hitting both.
\nBalder hits with his axe. He uses second wind to heal himself some.
\nThe giant misses.
\nMeena hits with both attacks.
\nModax hits with one attack.
\nBalder attacks but misses.
\nThe giant attacks Balder and takes him down. He attacks Meena, hitting her.
\nMeena hits and takes him down.
\nWe win round one.<\/p>\n

Round two are against a male and female humans in robes with mystical symbols.
\nThe man casts a spell on Balder. A fire bolt goes over his head.
\nThe female mage casts Magic Missile on Balder.
\nBalder attacks the f mage.
\nMeena moves to the f mage and hits both attacks.
\nModax casts Magic Missile on the f mage. She collapses.
\nThe M mage casts a spell on himself.
\nBalder misses his attack.
\nMeena misses her two attacks.
\nModax rages and attacks with his spear, twice, hitting both.
\nThe m mage looks at Meena and says \u201cyou should be helping me\u201d. Meena fails her will save.
\nBalder hits the mage. She also takes her second wind.
\nModax attacks twice, hitting once.
\nThe mage moves back. Modax hits with his AOO. The mage drops.
\nWe win the second round.<\/p>\n

Round three we face a blue slad and a gric.
\nModax attacks the gric with Magic Missiles.
\nThe gric moves up to Modax and attacks with his tail, hitting , but misses with his tentacle.
\nThe slag moves up to Balder and attacks with claws and teeth, but misses.
\nBalder attacks the slag, hitting him. He uses his action surge to attack again.
\nMeena hits the gric, hitting once.
\nModax casts Magic Missile on the gric.
\nThe gric attacks Modax, hitting with his tail. He misses with his tentacle.
\nThe slad attacks Balder, hitting him twice. Balder does not make his Con save. He feels a stinging sensation.
\nBalder misses the slad.
\nMeena hits the gric once.
\nModax casts Magic Missile on the gric.
\nThe gric moves up and attacks Modax with his tail. He misses with his tentacle attack.
\nThe slad attacks Balder, missing with his claws, and bite.
\nBalder, attacks the slad, hitting him.
\nMeena attacks the gric and takes him out.
\nModax casts chill touch, but misses.
\nThe slad attacks Balder, hitting him.
\nThe slad misses.
\nBalder, crits him.
\nMeena crits the slad.
\nModax rages and attacks hitting twice.
\nThe slad fumbles.
\nBalder misses his attack.
\nMeena hits once.
\nModax hits twice.
\nThe slad attacks Modax, but misses.
\nBalder attacks, hitting him.
\nMeena hits, taking him down.
\nWe win round three.<\/p>\n
