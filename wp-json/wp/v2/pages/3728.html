The Feywild Moon Spirit of Waele<\/p>\n

The GM may decide to provide an alternate set of adventure materials for characters looking to form Warlock Pact Bonds. <\/p>\n

Rivin is a spirit fo the Moon Spirit Waele – He is a special bull lycanthrope. He is almost always in his moon influenced minotaur form, and when he gets to leave the maze, during the full moon, he is a normal man, like to watch wrestling, enjoys good smoke and whiskey and has a normal wife. They lead a boring life, and he considers his time as Maze guardian his “job”. <\/p>\n

Unseelie vs. Seelie
\nUltimately Titania – the Queen of Summer – is the spirit’s arch-fey superior. Any pact would ultimately be with her, but the spirit of Waele will be an intermediary. <\/p>\n

Pact:<\/p>\n

Cost
\n1 Essence in addition (as long as there is a pact with his mistress) or 1 HP life essence permanently. <\/p>\n

Gain
\nThe spirit shrouds the PC in the border Feywild at night, and all HP regained due to rest are doubled as time passes strangely. <\/p>\n
