Prayer Done Right<\/p>\n

    \n
  1. The monks may seek out those who gained a blessing from the initial call to prayer will be sought out in down times by monks to show them a more disciplined way of prayer<\/li>\n
  2. Alternatively, monks of another order may also be sought out, simply as a fellow monastic.<\/li>\n
  3. Lastly, PC’s may visit the “Prayer Chapel” multiple times, just to pay respects and be noticed by the monks.<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n

    Characters with either wisdom or intelligence below 9 will not be ought out under any circumstances. <\/p>\n

    > If PC\u2019s make use of this blessing, and use it relax themselves while they are in a prolonged stay, the monks will add some meditation pointers. The way of prayer can be found in the index of the great library – with instructions on what, how and why the prayer is to be followed. Assuming the PC\u2019s essentially do it in the Prayer Chapel six times for 24 hours each time with monks overseeing the effort, they can earn a 1 pt Sanity increase from study costs 1 hp from intense githzerai meditation. They will have yellowed skin for year if they do this – a year when they return to the plane of their origin if they are not native. Non Githzerai cannot teach this to others, nor will it work outside the Prayer Chapel.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
