          <\/span>Sworn and Beholden<\/span><\/h3>\n

          This is a deep part of the Warlock – in exchange for knowledge and power, they serve their patron. At 4th, 8th, and 12th levels, all Warlocks voluntarily subjects themselves to a Gaes from their Patron. They must attempt to fulfill this Gaes as swiftly as possible, or lose the powers of their Eldritch Invocations. While under this Gaes, they gain the use of an additional Elritch Invocation to complete it, and when complete its use fades from the character’s ability.\n<\/p>\n

                  <\/span>Lesser Warlock<\/span><\/h2>\n

                  Sworn and Beholden: The character, in exchange for the immediate knowledge and power, voluntarily subjects themselves to a Gaes of their Patron. It cannot be to act against their own best interests, or harm their companions. The lesser Warlock is for characters wishing to bond themselves to a power in exchange for specific service. All such Lesser Warlock Pacts require the sacrifice 1 Essence and 1 HP permanently.<\/b><\/i><\/p>\n

                  <\/span>Lesser Warlock Pacts<\/span><\/h3>\n

                  Fighting Prowess<\/b>: For Fighters seeking Eldritch Knight<\/a> powers without having to train for a long time and learn their arcane skills. Such characters are not limited to the recommended cantrip\/spell selections of their archetype\/pathway. At 5th level, they can cast a Ritualized<\/a> Find Familiar – the creature type is that of the character’s patron. The familiar is nominally a spy for the patron as well, and will always be whispering promises of power, trying to advance further pacts and bonds. If killed, it cannot be summoned again until the character reaches the next level.\n<\/p>\n

The familiar is nominally a spy for the patron as well, and will always be whispering promises of power, trying to advance further pacts and bonds. If killed, it cannot be summoned again until the character reaches the next level.\n<\/p>\n
