History
\nBelim is a demobilized member of a company of dragoons from Dunstrand\u2019s militia (\u201cThe Queen\u2019s Own Tonbridge Dragoons\u201d). Common born, his father is a trader, traveling between Dunstrand and Oerdney (where the family business includes distant relatives). While his father intended him to work in the family trading enterprise, Belim was restless in that role and instead worked his way into the city guard of Oerdney, thence into the High King\u2019s army, and finally into the Tonbridge Dragoons. He is somewhat ambitious, as he believes proving one\u2019s loyalty to King and Country on the battlefield is the surest way for someone of his standing to advance to a higher station. His company is battlefield trained, but takes on a variety of odd tasks (some some interesting, some mundane) during relative peacetime; including activities such as espionage, security details, and scouting. He met his current companions on one of these \u2018assignments\u2019\u2014attempting to safeguard the reputation of a relatively minor but indiscreet noblewoman\u2014in the Dunstrand region.<\/p>\n

Belim and his unit have warred beside rangers in the past \u2013 and now a ranger has asked him to go to Bar-Innis. Much has been happening in what would normally be a completely out of the way place, beyond anyone\u2019s notice. However, too much has been happening there and the rangers are worried. Activity always means interest \u2013 and by who they want to know. More patrols of Orrish scouts have been followed into Dunstrand coming from the east\u2026 are they following the trail of interest, or are they reinforcements for a force already there? The court of the Earl of Bar-Innis is the pace to go. There are a group of bannermen that have sparked a lot of interest \u2013 seek them out and join their band. Find out about them and report back.
