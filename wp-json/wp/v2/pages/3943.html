Cherry Mops is the game everyone in Crow Alley looks forward to every year. The dyers guild gets rids of vast quantities of used dye batches in a mock festival of combat. Participants use mops and dunk them in the dye bins and in a semblence of blood, try and wollop their enemies. A man can have a stain on their front and one on their back before they have to leave. Teams are formed and one man fhas no map and carries a red ball – a cheap bundle of leather scraps died red. <\/p>\n

Each team has three people than are designated carriers – they have no mops. This “kettleman” tries to get to the west end of Crow Alley, and deposit the “ball” into a rusted ancient kettle. Only Kettlemen are able to touch the ball There is no end to the hijinx; though if your mop breks (many donated by the sweepers guild) you are out. This leads some teams to store mops in hiding places for later. <\/p>\n

This year the fake blood is going to cover up the real blood. Its been done before – but this is on racial lines now. <\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
