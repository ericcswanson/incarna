At its heart, a soul gem is nothing more than a crystalline binding agent. They are common in species that are connected to natural plants, minerals, etc. They are also commonly used as a means to focus and amplify Spark<\/a> power to interact with animism. <\/p>\n

Each individual species will have evolved a niche set of powers and functions for the soul gem. <\/p>\n

Regardless of any other function, the soul gem transfers 1 Essence from the body of the character into the Soul Gem. It does not decrease the Essence total of the character, it just alters how it stored. <\/p>\n

Common Powers Attributed<\/h2>\n

Powers can be automatic, or sometimes may require or consume for energy a part of the characters Essence total. <\/p>\n

Stabilizes in Death<\/b>: If the character drops to 0 HP, they automatically stabilize. It will not stave off Death Saves from any subsequent drops.\n<\/p>\n

Bonus to Death Save<\/b>: The character get a +1 (weak) or +2 (strong) bonus to their Death Saves.\n<\/p>\n

Flora Protection<\/b>: Immunity to secondary effects (non-damage) of cantrips associated with plants\n<\/p>\n

Essence Amplification<\/b>: +1 Essence\n<\/p>\n

Poisson Resistance<\/b>: Resistance to poison damage type\n<\/p>\n

Flora Obfuscation<\/b>: 1 Essence triggers the Pass Without Trace spell as a Bonus Action.\n<\/p>\n

Flora Communication:<\/b>: 2 Essence triggers the Speak with Plants spell as a Bonus Action.\n<\/p>\n

Creatures with Soul Gems<\/h2>\n

Faerie-Kin\/Elves<\/b> The Faerie-kind<\/a> are often associated with Soul Gems.
