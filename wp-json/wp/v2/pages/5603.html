    Those of the Pine Tribes speak an offshoot of ancient Dundarion<\/a> called “Dundrav” at a [Conversational] level. With this they can also speak Dundarion language<\/a> at [Rudimentary] level. Dundrav uses the same alphabet, has been simplified. It cannot communicate any idea beyond the [Understandable] level. Tribesmen are all familiar with the written form of this at [Primitive] level – they hide words in natural formations and obscure them with signs only they know of to ward off other tribes and help guide hunters and travelers of their own clan and tribe. <\/p>\n
