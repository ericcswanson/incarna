    “Everything Can be Found in Ados” is the motto of this thriving city. The city is perhaps the most open and accepting places in the realms. Its a haven for half-breeds, adventurers and mercenaries looking to make their fortune. The sheer volume of adventuring parties that assail the Darklands seeking out ruins and killing anything in their way is enough to offset the lack of a large standing army of Ados. Its massive population and thriving business as the first (and really only) major stop in the east of the south heartlands for anyone not going through Southgate Garrison makes it a crossroads of treasure and adventure seekers coming back from trips to the Darklands, the southern fallen empire and the rich mining that takes place in the Tolkisson Range.\n<\/p>\n
