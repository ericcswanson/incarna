Game Master = Gazza <\/p>\n

7\/15\/15
\nWe spend a week in the small town. Rax wants to sing every night. Meena spends 4 days making 4 potions of healing, 2 for Balder and 2 for Talia. Modax helps her.
\nMeena feels she must return to Portent Downs and report to Spider Brass. The others decide to go with her. We take a small boat to a larger town. We cannot enter the town as they have the plague. All food for our next passage on a larger ship to the mainland, is not going through town. They are being very careful not to cross contaminate. It is an inconvenience, but better than catching the plague. We are on the dock 4 days.
\nA large ship comes in and for 10 GP we book passage right away for Portent downs.
\nThe captain (Capt. Johnny Warhorse) asks to see us. He tells us they were not able to resupply in town, but there is plenty of ale. The ship is named \u201cThe Devil\u2019s Mistress\u201d.
\nWe notice there are a row of skulls tied on the side of the ship. We don\u2019t recognize them. Capt Warhorse tells us they are Sahaugin skulls. He assures us we will not be encountering them during this voyage.
\nIt takes about 2 weeks to get to Portent Downs. Rax and Meena entertain the sailors with music. Modax talks to the sailors. Talia spends much time in the crow\u2019s nest.
\nOn our 4th day, we are relaxing until the Captain yells, \u201cTo arms, to arms, battle stations!\u201d Meena, from the bow, sees several creatures swimming beneath the surface.
\nWe see 3 Sahaugin in the water. One throws a trident at Balder, but misses.
\nMeena tries to hit with a ballista on the bow, but not being familiar with the weapon, she misses.
\nModax climbs up from below deck.
\nWe see about 11 Sahaugin around the ship.
\nTalia comes down out of the crow\u2019s nest.
\nOne sahaugin starts to climb out of the water.
\nMeena shoots at one with her longbow, but misses.
\nModax casts magic missile and hits the one climbing next to Balderdash.
\nRax Eldritch blasts one.
\nTalia attacks, but misses.
\nOne attacks Balder, but he fumbles and falls face first on to the deck.
\nMeena moves down to the main deck and attacks one that climbed on to the ship. She hits with both sword attacks.
\nBalder attacks the one that fell in front of him and hits it.
\nModax casts Magic Missile at the one Balder just attacked. The sahaugin dies.
\nOne climbs up by Talia and takes a swipe at her, critting her with a claw attack for 6 pts.
\nOne attacks Meena as it climbs over the rail but misses both claw attacks.
\nAnother climbs up and attacks Balder, hitting with one claw.
\nRax misses with her Eldritch Blast.
\nTalia hits the one next to her.
\nMeena moves from between the two creatures and attacks one, hitting with the Sun Lord Sword.
\nBalder uses his breath weapon against 2 sahaugin.
\nModax casts Sleep on two sahaugin, but it has no effect on them.
\nTalia is hit for 2 more pts.
\nOne attacks Modax, but misses his spear attack.
\nBalder is hit a couple of times but he also fumbles and bites Balder\u2019s great axe.
\nTalia attacks, hitting.
\nMeena attacks the one next to her and takes him out.
\nBalder attacks hitting one next to him. He uses second wind to heal himself.
\nModax rages and attacks, hitting it.
\nOne attacks Talia, hitting with a claw, critting her.
\nOne hits Rax.
\nOne stabs at Modax, missing.
\nAnother targets Talia, but misses.
\nRax uses her breath weapon against 2 sahaugin.
\nTalia disengages and takes a potion.
\nMeena misses both her attacks.
\nBalder kills another one.
\nModax attacks but misses.
\nRax is hit again.
\nMeena is hit. She points at her attacker and casts Hellish Rebuke. He makes his Dex save but takes 13 pts of damage.
\nTalia steps back again and takes another potion of healing.
\nMeena attacks hitting once, but doing minimal damage.
\nBalder takes out the one that was just Rebuked.
\nOne attacks Talia, but fumbles.
\nOne crits Modax, but he takes half damage because of his Rage.
\nMeena attacks hitting twice.
\nModax takes out one.
\nMeena takes out the one next to her, then moves to the one by Talia and attacks, hitting him.
\nBalder crits against his attacker taking him out. He moves closer to Modax.
\nModax misses his attack.
\nOne misses Meena.
\nModax is hit for minimal damage.
\nRax hits one with her club, taking it down.
\nMeena takes one out, then she moves to another and crits him for max damage.
\nBalder crits the last one. He takes him out.
\nWe gain 200 XP\u2019s.
\n99 GP from all the sahaugin
\n4 art objects
\n4 gold bracelets (25 GP ea)
\nMeena-Nice +1 breastplate
\nModax-Spectacles with blue lenses (Eyes of Charming)
\nTalia-Nice pair of boots of Elvenkind
\nBalderdash-Nice pair of boots of Striding and Springing
\nStrand of Pearls (50 Pearls=100 GP ea) -3 for identifying our items.
