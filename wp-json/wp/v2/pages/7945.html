These were an elite band of scouts and way-finders, nearly a martial order but disliked the formality of such. At their helm was a Captain, often called the Summer Snow. There were 4 formal units, and that of the Winter Snow – the secretive enclave in the high peaks of the Tolkisson Range where the traditions and history was kept. The units never assembled unless at a time of war, and worked in pairs outside of that. The unit were the Red Roots, Misty River, Frost Spear, and Alder Brace. This pennon is the personal pennon of the Lt of the Frost Spear unit. It is coated with the viscera of a feral vampire that was killed as the unit was enveloped in the fall of the kingdom. The unit died to the last defending their countrymen.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
