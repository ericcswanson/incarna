Nurthrot is the name given to the effect that creates an effect that suppresses life magics and is inimical to the health and well being of the living. A mass Nurth suicide\/sacrifice pulls the veil between planes aside, and allows the taint of evil and death to spread like a miasma. It is based on the corrupted ritual of Djerduth.\n<\/p>\n

      <\/span>Cherokian’s Curse<\/span><\/h2>\n

      Originally it was seen in the Dwarven citadel of Steel Ring, in the Tolkisson mountains, south of North Gate Pass. When an army of skeletons struck frm the mountains, it was noted they were all Dwarven. A force went sent to the citadel guarding the Underlight path and it found the citadel overrun but no bodies. The dwarven priest Cheriokan could not identify it, it was simply dubbed “Cherokian’s Curse<\/b>“. Weeks later, the mass pile of decomposing pale bodies of what we be known as Nurth was discovered. It was considered some strange and evil ritual of sacrifice.\n<\/p>\n
