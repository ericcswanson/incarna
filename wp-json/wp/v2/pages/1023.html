    The story of ancient Nakria – known as The Nakreha – is lost of the myths of the past. There are few places recording the formation of the early “Empire”, other than to note that Empire was founded to protect the tribes from the encroaching pressure of migrations of the Blood of Saemon – referred to as “Outers”. The tribes are said to have lost their way then, and much history was lost as they fought among each other and to stem back the tide of the Outers.\n<\/p>\n
