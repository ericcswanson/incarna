    <\/span>Sacred Items<\/span><\/h2>\n

    <\/span>Kaleidoscopic Staff\/Bale Fire Shard<\/span><\/h3>\n

    <\/p>\n

    A piece of desert sand laced with Sun Stealer\u2019s blood and burnt by the fire of demons – fused into an obsidian shard that has wispy green flames licking off of it. Tt is also sometimes called the Kaleidoscopic Staff as it was used as a weapon by a powerful demon warrior (whose name is lost) in the time of The Sundering – lost in the great cataclysm, thought to be swallowed and crushed by the earth itself. It is about 7\u2019 long jagged pole-shaped chunk of obsidian, flecked with green. As the shard is moved, the flecks float about inside of it. either light or darkness is cast upon it, it causes all things around it to casts strange shadows, and a kaleidoscopic effect in the vision of all nearby – disorienting and captivating all at once. Thought to be destroyed, the item was carried off to The Deeping<\/a> by the Nurth, making an appearance at the Battle of Three Deep<\/a>, after which it was secreted away by the Ducateon to prevent its use in the world again (they could not destroy it).\n<\/p>\n
