A 3rd level evoker Gnome. This guy is basically the comic of the group, though he is nearly a sociopath. He exists to learn and catalog as much of magic as possible. He sticks with the group predominantly because Jack constantly leads them into new environments, and areas with different knowledge.<\/p>\n
