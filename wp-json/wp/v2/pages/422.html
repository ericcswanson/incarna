    The lands of the New West, Sayvalod, are also called “The Bitterlands”. They are so named for the Steel Realms inhabitants who see them as bitter and and weak. They have few of the terrors of the Steel Realms, and yet must pay tribute and send materials and soldiers to fight the forces of the Unholy Trio… and so are “bitter”. Sayvalod is the oldest, largest and most powerful kingdom of the Bitterlands.<\/b> The lands are referred to often by a reference to this single kingdom, There are hundreds of “Princes” across the lands. The main continent is referred to as Greatmaed, a corrupted form of “Great Made” – made by great beings – it was made during Morphoerastlynn’s Dream Scattering<\/a>.\n<\/p>\n

    Greatmaed is a land where the borders of worlds are often thin. There are several places on the east that are stable and where the races make their home. There are no Orrish (though many seek the power of the gods of the darkness), no Winter Host, and only abundance and the lands are verdant.\n<\/p>\n
