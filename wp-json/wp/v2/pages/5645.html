    “Fallen Sparrow” is the name given to fallen, neglected or forgotten shrines, churches and family chapels of the Lords of Light. The sparrow is the sacred bird to the Light Bringers and these far flung family chapels and shrines are the most remote points of their influence. These are often neglected due to abandonment, disuse, disrepair, or simply ignored and given lip service to. <\/p>\n
