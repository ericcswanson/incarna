10\/7\/15
\nWe take passage on a ship to get back to Portent Downs. As we disembark, Meena notices a squirrel, on the dock. It runs up to her shoulder and starts to talk to her. It says \u201cthis is a message from the head of the Guild. You must get this message to the Temple of Pelor. Tell the priests \u201cCold Fire\u201d. There are other parties making their way to the Temple as well. You must get there first.\u201d
\nAs we run down an ally, we run into a drunk who blocks our way. Balder and Meena do not get past him.
\nWe see someone from the docks running in the same direction. He yells at the drunk to get out of the way, and gets past him.
\nA woman who is running attacks Balder with a club, hitting him.
\nModax casts Magic Missiles against the woman who attacked Balder. She drops to the ground.
\nBalder tries to intimidate the drunk, and gets past.
\nMeena does not get past.
\nRax tries to grapple the drunk. He wriggles out of it and Rax stands with his dirty shirt in her hand.
\nTalia gets by.
\nModax casts Sleep on the drunk.
\nBalder attacks George, of the other party, hitting him.
\nMeena moves to the next area.
\nGeorge misses Balder.
\nTalia makes it through a short cut.
\nKara, another runner, can\u2019t get through the short cut.
\nModax casts Color Spray on Kara.
\nBarney attacks Balder, missing.
\nBalder attacks George taking him down.
\nMeena attacks Kara but misses both attacks.
\nRax does not make it through the short cut.
\nTalia comes across a pothole. She makes it past the pothole.
\nModax does not get past the short cut.
\nBarny attacks Balder, hitting him.
\nBalder hits Barney.
\nMeena misses her attacks against Kara again.
\nTalia finds a brick wall. She does not make it over.
\nKara attacks Meena, missing her.
\nModax flanks Barney and attacks with his spear, hitting him and taking him down.
\nBalder attacks Kara, hitting her.
\nA thug attacks Talia, missing her.
\nMeena attacks Kara, hitting her once.
\nRax uses Spider Climb to crawl around the Pothole.
\nTalia attacks the Thug with her rapier, hitting him.
\nKara misses Meena.
\nModax webs another runner.
\nMeena takes out Kara.
\nRax hits another runner, hitting her and knocking her back 10 feet.
