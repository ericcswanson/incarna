The Steeled Hand<\/h2>\n

This order of knights is NOT an officially recognized order, except for within the faith of Mizras. They operate to (as they put it) “balance out the scales”. They are often in the employ of hopeless causes, the underdog, or in service to a competing warlord or rival noble.
