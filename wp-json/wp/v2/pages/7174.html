Tactical #1
\nAdholf was meant to hunt and strike down enemies of the land. He is keen to always be on the side of life and and nature, and sees himself as a defender against the cancer and corruption of evil. As a warrior, he views himself as the one who must make the tough decisions, which sometimes seem crude or repugnant in fighting evil. He always goes for the most powerful of foes – groups if possible, using both weapons. He prefers for others to do direct engagement if he can maneuver to strike from the flank or behind in single foe fights. Against groups, he is the wall against which they break and will be in the middle.
