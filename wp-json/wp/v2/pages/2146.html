An orphan, born into the \u2018finkbonner\u2019 trader family, but left under the care of a family friend while mother, father and far elder brother went to the frozen north for what was supposed to be a highly profitable venture. The family friend was a veteran from the war with the gwinnish, and a zealous practitioner of Mizeras. War, conflict and rage describe childhood.<\/p>\n

The trade chosen is that of a blade for hire, as long as the client seems like a chump. This leads to the ring. Every match has silvers gambled on it worth as much as 50 times the prize money. Being the indestructible brute brings coins for nothing more than playing the part and striking non vital areas, and allows for massive gains with the occasional loss to the \u201ccourageous\u201d underdog. <\/p>\n

Life is fairly easy, wounds are minor when they happen at all, and the coins are more than need be spent. The regulars appreciate the fact that gentle nature and musical tendencies are the natural inclination, and the intimidation wrought from the ring discourages would be trouble makers.The song, the drink, the drugs, and the LADIES!!!!!!!!!!, can all be appreciated.<\/p>\n

Ego thwarts all. A fellow entertainer, of small stature and limited charismatic skill, issues public challenge. For the sake of the show the challenge is accepted, with the understanding that the challengers victory is imminent, and the blow must be convincing while allowing for a comeback. The blow is not false, threatening life. Time for recovery is needed, the show moves on.<\/p>\n

Pursuit of the show is possible, but seems empty. There have been no challenges of late. Even the one who brought ruin only managed a strike because it was allowed to happen. The ring is not the path any more. There are those here, they have seen and felt the pain. Perhaps they would appreciate the skills and tenacity presented.
