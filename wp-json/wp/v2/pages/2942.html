War of Sister’s Ascention<\/h2>\n

CY 9146, 2 sisters lay claim to the regency of the High King. The land is divided. Bejerrie Durgrimm, whose family home is in Ados, nearly begins a civil war. The D’avlin clan crushes the opposition and begins a six year campaign of over-taxation and oppressive secularism. She is killed months before the king comes to power (it is though to ensure she did not commit regicide) and the land returns to normal.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
