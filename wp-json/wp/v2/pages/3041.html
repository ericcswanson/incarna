The Serpentis sub-genre of dragon descendants are variants of serpents. They have a bodily serpentine element, but may have limbs and heads resembling other more humanoid species. Most dragons consider them a blasphemous offshoot; the particular humanoid\/serpent combinations produced spectacularly cunning sub species, with a decided lack of empathy for other species.
