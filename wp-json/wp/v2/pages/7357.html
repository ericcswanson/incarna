    Source: Conjure Woodland Beings<\/b>: <\/p>\n

    Movement: 30′ swimming \/ 30′ walking
    \nArmor Class: 14
    \nHD\/HP: 8D8\/36
    \nAlignment: Chaotic Evil
    \nCombat<\/b>: Multi-Attack: It attacks 3 times per round with 2 claws (@ +5; 2d6\/7) and a tendril (@ +5; 3d6\/10). It has 2 tendrils, one is used to stabilize it. A tendril is 10′ long, AC: 13 and can take 30 damage before being cut off.
