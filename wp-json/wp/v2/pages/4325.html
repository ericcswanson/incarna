    <\/a><\/div>\r\n\r\nAaismar characters are, generically, descendants of Celestials which manifested and mated with lesser creatures. True Aasimar – Direct descendants of the Celestial Patrons – have an alien cast to them, and a mindset that sets them apart from the Aaismar normal and heavily filtered descendants. <\/p>\n

    True Aaismar<\/h2>\n

    Animal Feature(s)<\/b>: Partial feature manifest – cat eyes, whiskers, ears, fur, fangs, etc. – tell tale signs of animal heritage.\n<\/p>\n

    True Inheritors<\/b>: The arrogance that goes with the species that uplifted mankind and the merovin.\n<\/p>\n

    Linked Parentage<\/b>: These have their parents and grandparents take a direct interest. Each 3 years they must seek them out in a place of solitude for a chance to discuss that they have learned and what it is to be genuinely divine, and progressing as a entity. At the age of 15, the character is given a humonculus in order to see how they have a ‘creation’ that they may uplift and guide themselves.\n<\/p>\n
