Characters are representations of an individual or creature in the game. Character creation follows the basics outlined in <\/span> ©Players Handbook<\/cite><\/a>, which follows common statistics<\/a> which express them in game terms, each character being a unique representation of the powers and effects they may enact through their Actions<\/a>. Character facets at the time of creation are chosen by the Player, the rest are gained through game play. Character facets are most often expressed in “levels” of achievement (skill, class, etc.). <\/p>\n

In Incarna, level advancement is based on story, not Experience Points.<\/mark> Levels are awarded by the Game Master to match the pace and flow of the story. They are based on game time spent, encounters and experiences, and benchmarks achieved in an adventure or campaign.<\/p>\n

Players are encouraged to advocate for their characters. <\/p>\n

Rewards<\/a> are also given in the form of such things as Inspiration<\/span>, Blessings, Boons, as well as Character Points<\/strong> (CP). Character Points can be used to gain knowledge (Lores<\/a>, languages, literacy), skills<\/a>, and Traits<\/a> in between levels that serve to customize a character.\n<\/p>\n
