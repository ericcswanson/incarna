Knight Quest<\/h2>\n

He dreams of becoming a knight! All Knights Are Bannermen. There are specific signs he is told he must have – knight must look and act (min. CHA 3) a certain way! SIGNS > Lore: Law (Rudimentary<\/a>; or maintain in their retinue one who does have this knowledge); Speak the language of the lord’s lands competently; Competent Lore of the lords lands; Ride – must be able to move adeptly across the lord’s lands; (expected) court clothes; Full Portion x3 jewelry <\/p>\n

Loosely obey the high code of chivalry:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Live to serve King and Country.<\/li>\n
  • Live to defend Crown and Country and all it holds dear.<\/li>\n
  • Live one’s life so that it is worthy of respect and honor.<\/li>\n
  • Avoid lying to your fellow man.<\/li>\n
  • Avoid cheating.<\/li>\n
  • Avoid torture.<\/li>\n
  • Obey the law of king, country, and chivalry.<\/li>\n
  • Administer justice.<\/li>\n
  • Exhibit self control.<\/li>\n
  • Show respect to authority.<\/li>\n
  • Exhibit courage in word and deed.<\/li>\n
  • Defend the weak and innocent.<\/li>\n
  • Crush the monsters that steal our land and rob our people.<\/li>\n
  • Never abandon a friend, ally, or noble cause.<\/li>\n
  • Die with valor.<\/li>\n
  • Always keep one’s word of honor.<\/li>\n
  • Always maintain one’s principles.<\/li>\n
  • Never betray a confidence or comrade.<\/li>\n
  • Avoid deception.<\/li>\n
  • Respect life and freedom.<\/li>\n
  • Exhibit manners.<\/li>\n
  • Be polite and attentive.<\/li>\n
