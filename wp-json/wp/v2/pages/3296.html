A failed priest is one that has lost their ability to connect with the divine because of some disgraceful behavior. It is not a Transgression directly against their faith per se, but a continual behavior that has caused them to fall out of favor with their deity. Things such as alcoholism, pedophilia, drug addiction or lewd and lascivious behavior.\n<\/p>\n
