Anwin Attacked<\/h2>\n

The duly appointed powers of Dunstrand have been struck by the Gallants in a slap to the face. The Gallants, known for their heroism in the far reach of the west of Dunstrand have seemingly dealt a blow to the stability of the realm when they attacked Anwin. Although he was attacked leaving the Duchy, there were witnesses to the devastation. He even used decoys when asked later about the attempt on his life. It is unknown what reasons the Gallants had for attacking the representative of the Twin River Council. Regardless neither the Duke or the High King’s forces are inclined to step-in. Many rumors about of long held enmity, deception and high handedness by the council, and even dark magics.
