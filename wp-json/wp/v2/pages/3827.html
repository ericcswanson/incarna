Sacred Harmonics<\/b>: The Ducateon<\/a> craftswman and religious authorities have created a singular effect based on acoustic tones and religious chants particularly formed with Ducateon physique. <\/p>\n

Understanding them: A Bard or a character with the singing capability can make an Insight DC 17 check. Success means they understand that the chamber they are in resonates deeply and sound can be used as a weapon.\n<\/p>\n

Raw
\nCommon
\nMastery<\/p>\n

Using them: Given the short time to understand, only 3 effects can be made – whispers\/\u201dshushing\u201d become like daggers when bounced off the walls (D3\/2 attacks; D4\/2 damage but with advantage), low tones are shields (+3 AC whole round and -1 damage\/+5 as a Reaction to 1 attack), and a scream may hurl vocal javelins (D6\/3) damage to screamer, hurls D6\/3 \u2018javelins\u2019 per Action up to 60\u2019 for D6\/4 damage. All use Sanity adjustment as a damage modifier.\n<\/p>\n
