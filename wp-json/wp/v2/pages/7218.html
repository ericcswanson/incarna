Its got 2 sails and is made mostly for coastal journeys, though it can make deep sea ones (but lacks the cargo capacity to be out for very long). The Cutters are made only in Loamwold using specific trees, by the halflings there. <\/p>\n

Only a single one is launched each year, and they are especially resilient and fast – perfect for blockade running and smuggling. <\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
