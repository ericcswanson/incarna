Game Master = Gazza
\n5-6-15
\nThere are two small passages in the south side of the room. Eera starts down the west corridor. She comes to a room with a cave in and lots of rubble.
\nWe work at clearing out the rubble, at Eera\u2019s request. After 10 hours, we are exhausted and we are no closer to finding out what caused the collapse.
\nWe take a long rest.
\nWe go down the East corridor, Eera in the lead. We find a room that is partially flooded with ice covering the water. There is a Sun Lord alter with a large Elk upon the altar, which is frosted.
\nEera approaches the altar and finds a gold amulet of the Sun Lord. She gives it to Singin to look at. Eera thanks the elk for its gifts and takes the elks antlers.
\nSingen gives the amulet to Meena to keep as party treasure.
\nEera finds a secret door. It is unlocked. Singen goes in to the room and finds 3 goblins.
\nDulug shoots at Singen for 4 pts.
\nGrog attacks Singen with a scimitar, missing.
\nSinjin casts Sleep. Two of the three fall asleep. Then he moves back into the other room. With a bonus action he casts Bardic Inspiration on Alta.
\nMeena tries to shoot with her longbow, but misses.
\nEera uses a thorn whip and hits Grog.
\nGrog crits Eera.
\nMeena misses again.
\nSinjin hits with his bow.
\nMeena finally hits for 6 pts.
\nEera attacks and takes out Grog. We also kill the other goblins.
\nIt looks like they have been living in this room. There is an altar with the skeleton of a boar. Eera takes the boar\u2019s skull.
\nWe find on the goblins:
\nNice scimitar; a human eyeball; a potion of disguise self; a potion of haste; 14 GP. We identify the scimitar as a +1 weapon the goblins called \u201cDog Slicer\u201d.<\/p>\n

Eera heads down the next passage way. There is a secret door. Eera hears scratching on the far side and tells the party about it. She prepares a spell. She opens the door. It is dark. She enters the room. In the room is a large skeleton of a wolf.
\nAlta enters the room and attacks the skeleton. She uses her Bardic Insp. to hit. And she hits on a second attack.
\nMeena hits twice with her swords.
\nEera takes him out.
\nThe altar in this room is covered in rubble. Eera takes the wolf\u2019s skull.
\nWe go through the main door and go into the room with the 7 doors. We have checked one side.
\nWe check the room at 11:00, but it won\u2019t open. We believe it is the room that we cleared the rubble from.
\nThe door at 9:00 only opens a crack.
\nSinjin unlocks the door at 7:00, but it only opens a crack.
\nWe move to the stairs, heading down. Sinjin leads us down. We come to a stone door with a sun on it. Sinjin suggests we try fitting the amulet in it. It fits, and the door slides open taking the amulet with it.
\nThere are urns, a couple treasure chests and weapons and armor lying about.
\nSinjin moves into the room checking for traps. He moves towards an urn. We hear the sound of bone against stone.
\nMeena moves into the room and crits one of the skeletons.
\nA skeleton hits Sinjin. Another hits him as well. He is down.
\nEena moves into the room and casts Thunderwave. Two of the skeletons collapse. The other two and the rest of the party are injured and blown 10 feet back.
\nMeena moves next to Sinjin and casts Cure Wounds, awakening him.
\nSinjin casts True Strike on Alta. She attacks and takes out a skeleton. She moves to the last skeleton and hits him too.
\nEera hits the skeleton and he crumbles.
\nWe do some healing and then take a long rest.
\nWe check the room for traps and check out the chests. One of them is empty, as is the other. The armor and weapons are rusty and ruined.
\n212 G; 230 S; 4 pearls (100 G); Longbow (old style) in perfect condition. The vases have no dust on them. They are empty.
\nSinjin identifies the Longbow as a +1 Longbow (Sinjin) using one of the pearls.
\nSinjin tells us the vases are glowing slightly. They are trolls that have had a transformation spell put on them about 1000 years ago. They have been conscience and they have a 15 Intelligence. Eera throws hers overboard. Meena keeps hers for now.<\/p>\n

We find a chink in the wall and find a ring. It is a Life ring with a sun, star, and moon on it. It gives you 20 temp hit pts when worn.
\nWe take our treasure and make our way back out of the mountain. We head back to the lake where the ship is waiting.
\nEera buries the remains of the sacrifices.
\nWe board the ship and we head out.
\nWe meet with Spider Braz. He is pleased with our efforts. He pays us the 500 GP each.
\nMeena gives 100 GP to her family in town.
\nSinjin buys a breast plate.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
