Joonzey was originally a member of the Humma elite forces. His unit was wiped out during a Sathar raid, and only he and three others survived behind enemy lines. There was cannibalism and horrific ordeals for over 30 days before he was rescued. He could not integrate into the normal military units after that. He was sent to Star Law psi-ops to try and heal his mind and calm him down; it took a year before he was able to serve with any group again.
