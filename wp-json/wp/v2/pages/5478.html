80% of the city of Potash is so lawless that pretty much any weapon can be carried in the open (or concealed) on the streets. So it’s pretty much a criminals’s dream come true. The police, if even in attendance, wouldn’t bat an eye; only the gangs might cast a wary eye – they’re the ones really in control. There’s still a ‘Green Zone’ where law prevails and non-scum have their restaurants, hotels, and offices – you’ll probably want to stay there unless you’re feeling particularly frisky. Also there’s a really good BBQ place up in Dogsbottom. 4.7 stars on IshakBook. <\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
