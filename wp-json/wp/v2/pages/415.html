    Law: High Kings, Dundaria Kings, cultural traditions taken as law.
    \nDefenses: Military, Mountain Stewards of Dundaria
    \nMilitary:
    \nThe king’s soldiers have several rallying points throughout the kingdom, and several small keeps that serve to protect trade routes and supply storage and staging. Each noble is expected to quarter a garrison in every major city, and provide for it. The military is mostly volunteers, but all noble families are required to send their children for 2 years of service – be it logistics, medicine, or fighting. <\/p>\n
