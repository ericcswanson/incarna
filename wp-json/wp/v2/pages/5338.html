Kayle: A 3rd level half elf wild magic sorcerer. Kayle was an urchin in the city of ‘Montvin’ (the city we just spoke of. He was a thief and in the revolution acted as an informant to Jack. When he started aiding Jack his powers manifested, some kind of weird tie to his elven ancestry. He feels that he owes finding meaning in his life to Jack, so he followed him when the revolution failed. Jack seems to trust him above all others, though it is apparent to everyone else that Kayle is still a rogue at heart. Even his spells are predominantly utility\/rogue based.<\/p>\n
