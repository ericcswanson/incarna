          Interpose = Item can be used to block\/deflect a blow. Any item with Resilience<\/a> can be sacrificed to reduce the effect of a blow to half damage (no additional effects are reduced), at the cost of the item. A better than average hit is reduced to an average hit, and an average or less is reduced to no damage and no physical effect.
