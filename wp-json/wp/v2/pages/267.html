    The High-King of the realms holds the seat of the kingdom of Gladnor in addition to the title of High King. Several reasons make this territory as important, but over the years, the title has come to mean less. However, the coordinated defense of the realms is the duty still, and the high king and his designates still hold the most power of any single lord. <\/p>\n
